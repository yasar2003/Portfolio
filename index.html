Ui/Ux Design

UI/UX design focuses on creating user-friendly and visually appealing digital experiences. UI (User Interface) design deals with the look and feel of a product, including buttons, typography, and layouts. UX (User Experience) design ensures seamless navigation, accessibility, and user satisfaction. Tools like Adobe XD, Figma, and Sketch help designers build intuitive, responsive, and engaging websites or mobile applications.
